About Me

Hello! My name is Jacob and I am a 31 year old video game programmer/student that is currently studying Video Games Programming at the University of Gloucestershire. I have had a passion for video games since I was very young after I recieved a PlayStation 2 and Crash Bandicoot for Christmas in 2002 and I have been completely enamoured with making games since. I have a particular fondness for creating the most fun gameplay systems that I possibly can through game jams and general mucking about within game engines. I am always eager to learn something new and always ready to accept any critiques to further my understanding and improve on what I make. I also greatly enjoy working within a team to create work that all of us could be proud of.
